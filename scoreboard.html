Scoreboard

"Days since..." counters. These update automatically based on today's date — you never need to do the maths yourself.

✏️ Editing this page

Open js/data.js on GitHub and find the SCOREBOARD array. Each card has a label and a lastDate.

When something happens again (e.g. you have a drink), just change that card's lastDate to that day's date in "YYYY-MM-DD" format and commit. The counter resets itself.
